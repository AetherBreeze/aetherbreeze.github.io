The Elephant In The Room

Before I go any further, I should mention a few important things. The first is something observant readers might have noticed. Look at the constant in the path integral‚Äôs definition:

Some people believe these are deep truths hiding in the use of Wick rotations. I don‚Äôt know enough to be one of them, but I would be remiss if I didn‚Äôt point it out.
